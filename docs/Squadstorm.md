# Technical Task (TZ)

**Project:** Squadstorm
**Concept:** A modern mobile spiritual successor to the legendary Cannon Fodder (1993), reimagined as a Roguelike Twin-Stick Shooter in the visual style of Brawl Stars.

## Core Vision & Legacy
* **The Original Spirit:** The game must retain the "easy to play, hard to master" feel of the original Cannon Fodder.
* **The Hook:** "War has never been so much fun"—combine the dark irony of the 1993 classic with colorful, juicy 3D visuals.
* **The Twist:** Add modern Roguelike progression (randomized perks during the run) and Co-op play.

## UI & Layout Mockups (Landscape Orientation)
**CRITICAL:** The game is played strictly in **Landscape (Horizontal)** mode. All UI mockups, menus, and gameplay screens must be horizontally oriented.
* **Left side of screen:** Movement Joystick (Left Stick) for tactical movement (dodging, flanking).
* **Right side of screen:** Aiming Joystick (Right Stick) for manual aiming.
* **Auto-Fire:** Soldiers fire automatically when the right stick is pointed toward an enemy.
* **Top Screen:** Mission timer, score, and current squad status/health.
* **Bottom Screen:** Active skills, revive buttons, or quick-access items.

## Gameplay Mechanics
* **Squad Management:** You control a squad (3-5 soldiers). They move as a unit but have individual health bars.
* **Permadeath (The Fodder Element):** If a soldier dies, they are gone for the rest of the run. At the end of the mission, new "recruits" join to fill the gaps, but they start without the veteran bonuses.

## Roguelike Loop & Progression
* **Run Structure:** 5-minute missions across procedurally generated maps (Jungle, Desert, Snow).
* **Level-Up Perks (During Run):**
  * *Heavy Ordinance:* Grenades deal +50% area damage.
  * *Medic Pack:* Passive health regeneration when out of combat.
  * *Tactical Spread:* Squad fires in a wider cone.
* **Meta-Progression (The Base):** Between runs, upgrade your "Boot Camp" to increase starting squad size or unlock permanent air-strikes.

## Visual Style & Co-op
* **Style:** Low-poly 3D with "Bouncy" animations. Vibrant colors but with the classic "tombstone" icons when soldiers perish.
* **Co-op (The "Buddy" System):**
  * Two players on screen.
  * Player A manages the Infantry, Player B manages the Heavy Support (Tanks/Mortars).
  * Shared "Hill of Fame"—the more you play together, the bigger your shared memorial for fallen heroes.

## Monetization (Hybrid-Casual)
* **Free-to-play:** Main revenue through "Revive" ads (limit 1 per run) and Battle Pass skins for soldiers (different uniforms, hats, and muzzle flashes).
